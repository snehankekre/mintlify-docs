## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sources">
    Go to the Sources section in your AstroBee dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose connection type">
    Select BigQuery for database connections or upload CSV files
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure connection">
    Provide necessary credentials and connection details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and connect">
    Verify the connection and start exploring your data
  </Step>
</Steps>

## BigQuery Setup

To connect your BigQuery data:

1. **Authentication**: Provide your Google Cloud credentials
2. **Project selection**: Choose your BigQuery project
3. **Dataset access**: Select which datasets to connect
4. **Permissions**: Ensure AstroBee has read access to your data

## CSV Upload

For quick data exploration:

1. **File upload**: Drag and drop or select your CSV files
2. **Data preview**: Review the data structure and column types
3. **Schema detection**: AstroBee automatically detects data types
4. **Import**: Complete the upload to start querying
