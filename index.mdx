[AstroBee](https://astrobee.ai/) is the first AI agent that builds your ontology for you. Your enterprise data questions - answered. Get ad hoc insights in minutes, not days, without blocking on a busy data team.

AstroBee revolutionizes how organizations interact with their data by automatically building intelligent ontologies that understand your business context. Instead of waiting days or weeks for data teams to answer questions, get instant insights through natural language queries.
